<a href="https://documentation.ai/docs/customize/access-control#access-modes" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">**Access modes**</a>

<a href="http://Documentation.AI" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Documentation.AI</a> supports three access modes:

- **Public**: Anyone can access your documentation. No authentication required.

- **Private**: All pages require authentication to access.

- **Partial**: Make some pages public and restrict access to others.
